Tactical PIP & Map

戦術盤

The glowing circular viewport is your Picture-in-Picture and 2D radar map. Click it to swap between FPV and Top-Down, or tap the MAP button on the sidebar HUD.

Left & Right HUD Wings

両翼

Two lateral panels frame the action. Left holds your card decks, slot trackers, and movement D-Pad. Right shows levels, vitals, and instant action dials.

Grid Navigation

移動

Walk and turn with the D-Pad in the Left Panel or your keyboard Arrow keys. Tap anywhere inside the 3D viewport to face and attack a target monster instantly.

Tactical Thumb Button

親指

The large circular avatar in the Right Panel is your tactical thumb button. Its colored bezel shows real-time health. Click the avatar to cycle active card decks.

Action Panel Zone

操作

The Right Panel's octagonal Action Panel toggles Modes (FPV / Top-down), opens your Bag, surfaces settings, and saves / loads snapshots of your current floor.

Action Chat Input

対話

Type into the Chat Input at the bottom. Press Enter to fire commands, launch projectiles, or parley with monster merchants.

Event & Narration Log

記録

The scrolling log on the bottom-left tracks real-time combat stats, loot pickups, and atmospheric narration straight from the Oni-Baba Grudge Director.
